About Our Salon and Spa

For the Chnapko women, Bernard’s at Brighton is the phoenix rising from the ashes. Opening a new spa and salon was a dream that began during Bernie “Bernard” Chnapko’s battle with cancer.

Upon his death, it became a venture that Elizabeth, his wife, and Maria, the youngest of three daughters, were inspired to bring to fruition. Today, Elizabeth, Annette, Michelle and Maria are dedicated to making Bernard’s at Brighton the premier Day Spa and Salon in Lancaster County.

Our Mission

To provide undivided personal attention in a wholistic environment that will enhance the physical and mental well-being of our clientele.

Spa Expectations

* Your Level of Comfort: The best way to receive a massage or other bodywork is to be unclothed. However, you may disrobe to your comfort level. During all treatments, the only area of the body that is not completely draped is the area being attended. Your privacy will be upheld at all times.

* Personal Preferences: Please communicate your preferences with your provider. Whether it is the room temperature or the level of pressure, let the therapist know immediately that you are uncomfortable. Our goal is for your experience to be positive, and corrections can only be made if we are made aware of a problem at the time of service.

* Satisfaction Statement: If you have suggestions or if we did not meet your every expectation, please call the manager. We will ensure you receive full satisfaction.
