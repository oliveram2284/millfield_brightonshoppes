Bernard’s at Brighton

A Day Spa and Salon. Serving the Lancaster, PA area, we offer a holistic environment that will enhance your physical and mental well-being. Our services include massage therapies, skin care, waxing, nail care and hair design.

Indulge yourself at Bernard’s at Brighton where old-world luxury awaits. Our staff is anticipating your arrival, upon which you will be escorted to one of our service areas.

Our salon offers a make-up area, six styling stations, two private color stations and two hair washing stations. Design services are available for both men and women.

Our spa is comprised of two massage rooms with a pocket door to also accommodate couples’ services. Two rooms are designated for aesthetician services. Each of our men’s and women’s locker rooms opens to serenity rooms, where you can enjoy tea while you relax. Our spa services ensure your total well-being.
