Bikram Yoga at Brighton is a beginner's yoga series, based on the Hatha Yoga principles. Bikram Yoga is a fun, demanding and physically-challenging 26-posture series that works the entire body - from the inside out. Every posture, or asana, is intended to be performed in the given order of the sequence, to the best of one's ability.

A principle of the class is that physical exertion and relaxation operate most effectively when practiced simultaneously through controlled breathing and focus.

The classes are 90-minute sessions and performed in a heated room.
