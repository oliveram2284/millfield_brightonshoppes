We are a locally owned BYOB Art Studio located in Lancaster County, Pennsylvania. Our goal is to provide you with an EXCITING, CREATIVE and FUN experience.

Enjoy an evening of creativity, drinking and socializing with your friends while a local professional artist guides you through an acrylic canvas painting design. There is no experience necessary and upon completion of the class you will be taking home a self-created work of art that you can be proud of! Simply bring yourself and a choice of drink(s) and/or food and we'll supply the rest.

PUBLIC PAINTING CLASSES are held regularly during the evenings. The majority of our classes are 2 hours long. Check out the Calendar for specific class information.

FAMILY DAY CLASSES are open to parents and children of all ages. Check out the Calendar for specific class information.

PRIVATE PAINTING PARTIES & EVENTS are available for scheduling any day of the week as long as the studio is available. There are NO age restrictions for our Private Painting Parties.  You can even have your child's birthday party with us!

Our BYOB classes are for adults.  Be prepared to show I.D. 

YOU BRING: Yourself, drinks, food, ice. THATS IT!

WE PROVIDE: All painting supplies, napkins, drink ware, plastic ware, corkscrew and bottle opener.
