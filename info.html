Brighton Village Shoppes

Brighton, the first Traditional Neighborhood Development (TND) to be created in Central PA, was founded in 1997. Brighton is conveniently located in Manheim Township, Lancaster County, 3 miles north of the City of Lancaster on Fruitville Pike, across from Bent Creek Country Club. The Brighton Village Shoppes, when the second phase is completed, will include over 23,000 square feet of retail space.

Location is crucial in any retail environment, in today’s economy, it is especially important to be located in an appealing, attractive, busy area. Brighton Village Shoppes have ample on-street & off-street parking, high volume traffic, surrounded on all sides by Lancaster County’s highest income residential areas.…. Existing businesses include a dry-cleaner, a full service day spa, dental office, a bakery, gift shop, and personalized exercise training.

Phase One has an established mix and synergy of creative retail, professional and service oriented businesses. Phase Two development plans are approved and we are looking for lease commitments to begin construction. We are looking to expand the retail mix with destination type businesses. Possible uses include: restaurants, floral service, a pharmacy, a chiropractor &/or optician along with retail of gifts and apparel. Square footages range from 1,000 sq. ft. - 5,000 sq. ft. and can be built to your interior specifications!
