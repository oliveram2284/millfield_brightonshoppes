Love Give Live (LGL) is a newly formed nonprofit development organization committed to relieving suffering and promoting wholeness. We are registered with the state of Pennsylvania and the IRS as a tax-exempt 501(c)(3) corporation. LGL headquarters is located in Lancaster County, Pennsylvania.

LGL creates innovative business ventures with the following two purposes:
- Generate ongoing revenue streams for non-profit organizations that share LGL's passions and purpose
- Connect people with information and opportunities to promote growth and wholeness
This world is a tough place. All of us, to some degree, are broken and have experienced suffering. Some, however, experience extreme brokenness and suffering and need others to step in and help. Love Give Live recognizes there are many organizations already doing great work, relieving suffering and promoting wholeness. LGL provides additional resources for them to be able to do more of the great work they are already doing. Because the needs are so many and so great, LGL has chosen to select organizations working to relieve suffering and promote wholeness in areas near and dear to us. All organizations that LGL supports must meet very high standards of integrity and transparency. The entire vision for LGL is motivated by the faith of its Founders, James H. Carson, MD, and his wife Gail Graybill-Carson.

 

LGL believes every person has a purpose, is gifted with unique skills and abilities, and has certain things they are passionate about. Life is at its best when our passions align with our purpose and our gifts. We believe our purpose is no less than a divine appointment. We believe that God has created each and every person with unique passions and gifts and that He is passionately in love with His creations.
